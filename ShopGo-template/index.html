Welcome to Shop & Go

We aim to provide our customers the best experience while shopping at a store. Planning out routes throughout the store of your choice saving you time wandering the store for your item.

About Us

This idea was founded by Software Engineer Tyler Sackel, with the help of colleagues such as Caleb Marcellus to help bring his ideas to life. His aim was to make shopping as easy as possible for customers without intruding on their privacy.

Our Services

The starting features of the application include:

  • A Grocery List feature for customers to organize their items.
  • A Map feature to find the best route in a store.
  • A Profile section to customize user preferences.
